SUICIDAL RATES
According to WHO, more than 700,000 people die due to suicide every year. For every death there are many people who attempt suicide.
Suicide is the 4th leading cause of death among 15-19 year olds.

What is the purpose of this project? To deduce our own observations from the statistical manipulation of the dataset with the hope of answering the below questions:
1. What distribution of ages, sexes is most affected by suicide
2. Which country is most and least affected by suicide
3. Has the magnitude of suicide deaths changed overtime? if so, how much?
4. The suicide frequency overtime.
